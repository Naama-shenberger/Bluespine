Rule Name: Clinical Consultation Request

Description: Clinical consultation services are requested by the patient's attending physician

SQL-based implementation:

SELECT * FROM ClaimLines WHERE ProcedureCode IN ('80503', '80504', '80505', '80506') AND ClaimNumber IN (SELECT ClaimNumber FROM Claims WHERE NPI IN (SELECT NPI FROM ProviderInfo WHERE Specialty = 'Pathology')) -- REQUIRES MANUAL REVIEW OF DOCUMENTATION
