Rule Name: Gynecological Exam and E&M Performed on the Same Day

Description: A gynecological examination (G0101, S0610, S0612, or S0613) and an E/M service (99202 through 99215, 99381 through 99387, 99391 through 99397) may be reported on the same day if the patient's records contain sufficient documentation regarding the appropriateness of performing both services and documentation of the key components that the service(s) were met.

SQL-based implementation:

SELECT * FROM ClaimLines WHERE ProcedureCode IN ('G0101', 'S0610', 'S0612', 'S0613') AND EXISTS (SELECT 1 FROM ClaimLines cl2 WHERE cl2.ClaimNumber = ClaimLines.ClaimNumber AND cl2.ProcedureCode IN ('99202', '99203', '99204', '99205', '99212', '99213', '99214', '99215', '99381', '99382', '99383', '99384', '99385', '99386', '99387', '99391', '99392', '99393', '99394', '99395', '99396', '99397') AND cl2.DOS = ClaimLines.DOS);

Rule Name: Pap Smear on the Same Day as Gynecological Examination or E/M Service

Description: A pap smear (Q0091) is not eligible as a distinct and separate service when performed on the same day as a gynecological examination (G0101, S0610, S0612, or S0613) or E/M service (99202 through 99215, 99381 through 99387, 99391 through 99397).
